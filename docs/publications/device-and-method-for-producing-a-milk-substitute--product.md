# Summary
{: .d-inline-block }
01-24-2026
{: .label .label-green }

The invention relates to a device for producing a milk substitute product, comprising a container with an inner shell defining a cavity and an upper opening for receiving the ingredients required for producing the milk substitute product and an outlet for removing the ready-to-eat milk product, a lid which is arranged on the opening of the container, a stirring device for stirring and dispersing the ingredients received in the container, wherein the stirring device comprises at least one driven, rotatable stirring element which is arranged in the cavity. The device further comprises a cleaning device for the automated cleaning of at least the inner shell of the container, and a heating device for heating the inner shell of the container. The lid further comprises at least one first inlet through which a mixture of ingredients can preferably be fed into the cavity of the container, wherein the lid comprises a closure device with a closure element for selectively opening and closing the first inlet. A device for producing a milk substitute product which is designed to save installation space and is cost-effective and enables the production of a ready-to-eat fresh milk substitute product in a time- and energy-saving manner is created in that the closure element can be adjusted via a drive motor between a closed position in which the closure element closes the inlet and an open position in which the closure element opens the inlet.
